## Title: Radio

## Data Type: Collection of Tracks & Sets

## Fields Abstraction

General:

- title
- slug
- description
- image

Music Specific:

- Wether it's a track or it's a set.
- artist
- genres
- releaseDate
- BPM
- key
- duration

- Source Types:
  Internal Upload (S3)
  External Platforms (SoundCloud, YouTube, Spotify, Beatport, Bandcamp)
